Our Services

Comprehensive Cardiac Care Solutions

Heart Life Hospital offers a comprehensive range of cardiac care services, from preventive care to advanced surgical procedures. Our state-of-the-art facilities and expert medical team ensure you receive the highest quality care for your heart health needs.

Diagnostic Services

  • Electrocardiogram (ECG/EKG)
  • Echocardiography
  • Stress Testing
  • Holter Monitoring
  • Cardiac CT and MRI

Interventional Cardiology

Our interventional cardiology department specializes in minimally invasive procedures to treat heart conditions:

  • Coronary Angiography
  • Angioplasty and Stenting
  • Valve Repair Procedures
  • Cardiac Catheterization

Cardiac Surgery

Our surgical team performs a wide range of cardiac procedures:

  • Coronary Artery Bypass Surgery
  • Heart Valve Surgery
  • Minimally Invasive Heart Surgery
  • Complex Aortic Surgery

Preventive Care

  • Cardiovascular Risk Assessment
  • Lifestyle Counseling
  • Nutrition Consultation
  • Cardiac Rehabilitation Programs
